## Photography Guidelines:

### Lighting & Time of Day
- **Golden Hour**: Sunset photos (warm, orange glow)
- **Blue Hour**: Just after sunset (deep blue sky, city lights)
- **Night**: Full dark with city lights, street lamps
- **Avoid**: Harsh daylight photos - they lack VIP prestige

### Vehicle Requirements
- **Color**: Black, dark grey, or dark blue ONLY
- **Condition**: Spotless, polished, reflective
- **Branding**: NO taxi signs, NO company logos on exterior
- **Style**: Unbranded premium vehicles (Mercedes, BMW, Audi)

### Composition Tips
- Shoot from slight low angle for dominance
- Include city skyline or modern architecture
- Use reflections (wet streets, building glass)
- Soft focus background, sharp vehicle
- Professional editing with color grading

### Technical Specs
- **Format**: WebP preferred (smaller file size)
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920x1080px
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 4:3
- **File Size**: Optimize to 200-500KB per image
- **Quality**: High resolution, sharp focus
