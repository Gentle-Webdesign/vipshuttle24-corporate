## Image Guidelines:

- **Format**: WebP preferred (better compression), JPG/PNG acceptable
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional photography
- **Aspect Ratios**: 
  - Hero images: 16:9 or wider
  - Service images: 4:3 or 16:9
- **File Size**: Optimize for web (50-500KB per image)
- **Content**: Showcase Mercedes-Benz vehicles, luxury details, professional atmosphere

## Quick Tips:

- Use online converters to convert JPG/PNG to WebP
- Compress images before upload (use tinypng.com or similar)
- Ensure images are well-lit and sharp
- Avoid watermarks or text overlays
